How to Study Offline

Safari (iOS)

  1. Go to the Spanish-Quizzer homepage.
  2. Tap on either Conjugations, Vocab, or the Reference Tables.
  3. Tap the share icon and select "Add to Reading List".
  4. Open the Settings app and select "Safari".
  5. Scroll down to the "Reading List" section and enable "Automatically Save Offline".
  6. When you are offline, open Safari, go to your Reading List, and tap on Spanish-Quizzer.

Firefox

  1. Go to the Spanish-Quizzer homepage.
  2. Click on either Conjugations, Vocab, or the Reference Tables.
  3. When you are offline, click on the hamburger menu in the top right corner, click on "Web Developer", and enable "Work Offline".
  4. Return to the page you clicked on to study offline.
  5. When you get back online, you must disable "Work Offline" in order to browse the internet again.

Chrome / Edge

Offline studying is not supported on Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge. Spanish-Quizzer will not work if it is saved as an HTML document.
