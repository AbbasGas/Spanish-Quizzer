Spanish-Quizzer

How to study offline

Safari (iOS)

  1. Go to the Spanish-Quizzer homepage.
  2. Tap the share icon and select "Add to Reading List".
  3. Open the Settings app and select "Safari".
  4. Scroll down to the "Reading List" section and enable "Automatically Save Offline".
  5. When you are offline, return to the homepage to study offline.

Chrome / Firefox / Edge

  1. Go to the Spanish-Quizzer homepage.
  2. Click on the star on the right side of the address bar.
  3. Click "Done".
  4. When you are offline, return to the homepage to study offline.
